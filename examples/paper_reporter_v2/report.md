# Applications of LLM-based Agents in Biology and Medicine

## Relevant Papers

1. **Generative AI agents are transforming biology research**  
   - **Authors:** Stefan Harrer, Rahul V. Rane, Robert E. Speight  
   - **Journal:** eBioMedicine  
   - **Publication Date:** November 8, 2024  
   - **Abstract:** This commentary discusses key applications of generative AI in biology, emphasizing improvements in function prediction and multimodal generative models for biological system studies.

2. **Large Language Models in Healthcare**  
   - **Author:** Antonio Nucci  
   - **Abstract:** Explores the transformational impact of LLMs in enhancing diagnostics, clinical documentation, and patient interactions in healthcare.

3. **Generative AI in Medical Practice: In-Depth Exploration of Privacy and Security Challenges**  
   - **Authors:** Yan Chen, Pouyan Esmaeilzadeh  
   - **Journal:** Journal of Medical Internet Research  
   - **Publication Date:** February 2024  
   - **Abstract:** Examines generative AI's applications in diagnostics and its associated security and privacy threats, proposing strategies for mitigation.

4. **Applications of LLMs in Patient Care**  
   - **Author:** Juan Martinez  
   - **Publication Date:** February 27, 2024  
   - **Abstract:** Focuses on LLM applications such as patient note automation and clinical decision support, enhancing communication and improving patient outcomes.

5. **Med-PaLM: A Medical Large Language Model**  
   - **Authors:** Various  
   - **Publication Date:** July 2023  
   - **Abstract:** Describes Med-PaLM, a model designed to deliver accurate medical information and its success in surpassing human-level accuracy for medical queries.

6. **Large Language Models in Drug Discovery and Development**  
   - **Authors:** Yizhen Zheng et al.  
   - **Publication Date:** September 6, 2024  
   - **Abstract:** Discusses the integration of LLMs into the drug discovery process and their role in understanding disease mechanisms and optimizing clinical trials.

7. **Emerging opportunities in Using LLMs for Drug Molecule Translation**  
   - **Authors:** David Oniani et al.  
   - **Journal:** Scientific Reports  
   - **Publication Date:** May 10, 2024  
   - **Abstract:** Proposes using LLMs to translate between drug molecules and their indications, aiming to benefit drug discovery.

8. **Generative AI in Drug Discovery and Development**  
   - **Authors:** Chiranjib Chakraborty et al.  
   - **Journal:** Annals of Medicine and Surgery  
   - **Publication Date:** August 14, 2024  
   - **Abstract:** Highlights the applications of generative AI in various stages of drug discovery and development and the associated efficiency gains.

9. **Unlocking the Power of NLP in Bioinformatics**  
   - **Publication Date:** September 15, 2023  
   - **Abstract:** Discusses NLP applications in bioinformatics, detailing methods like text mining and enhancing biological predictions.

10. **Harnessing Artificial Intelligence to Improve Clinical Trial Design**  
    - **Authors:** Bin Zhang et al.  
    - **Journal:** Communications Medicine  
    - **Publication Date:** December 21, 2023  
    - **Abstract:** Reviews the role of AI in optimizing clinical trial design and addressing biases.

11. **Artificial Intelligence in Medicine**  
    - **Authors:** Various  
    - **Journal:** New England Journal of Medicine  
    - **Publication Date:** March 29, 2023  
    - **Abstract:** Discusses AI applications in various medical areas, emphasizing the importance of ethical considerations.

12. **Generative AI for Medical Research**  
    - **Authors:** Jessica Morley et al.  
    - **Journal:** BMJ  
    - **Publication Date:** July 12, 2023  
    - **Abstract:** Explores the benefits and risks of generative AI in medical contexts.

13. **Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare**  
    - **Journal:** Wikipedia  
    - **Publication Date:** January 18, 2025  
    - **Abstract:** Outlines the applications of AI in healthcare, focusing on enhancing diagnosis and patient management.

14. **Unleashing the Power of AI in Healthcare**  
    - **Authors:** Hawaii Medical College  
    - **Publication Date:** November 8, 2024  
    - **Abstract:** Discusses how AI can improve diagnostics, treatment plans, and healthcare delivery.
